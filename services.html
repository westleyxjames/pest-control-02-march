Ant Control & Treatment

Ants are among the most common pest complaints in Hudson Falls. Different ant species require different treatment approaches. We identify the specific ant species affecting your property and apply targeted treatments to address both the visible ants and the colonies.

Common Ant Species We Treat:

  • Carpenter Ants: Can damage wood structures; require specialized treatment
  • Pavement Ants: Nest in cracks and foundations; common in driveways
  • Odorous House Ants: Attracted to sweets; form trails in kitchens
  • Pharaoh Ants: Small, difficult to control; require baiting systems

Our Treatment Approach:

  • Species identification to determine appropriate treatment
  • Strategic bait placement for colony elimination
  • Perimeter and crack/crevice treatments
  • Follow-up service recommendations based on species

Timeline: Most ant treatments show results within 3-7 days as the bait is transferred throughout the colony. Carpenter ant treatments may require 2-3 weeks for full effectiveness.

Rodent Management & Exclusion

Rodent problems are particularly common in Hudson Falls during fall and winter months when mice and rats seek indoor shelter. Our rodent management program focuses on both immediate control and long-term prevention through exclusion techniques.

Comprehensive Rodent Services:

  • Thorough Inspection: Identify entry points, nesting areas, and activity levels
  • Strategic Trap Placement: Tamper-resistant stations in key locations
  • Entry Point Documentation: Photos and detailed notes of access points
  • Exclusion Recommendations: Professional advice on sealing and prevention
  • Follow-up Monitoring: Regular check-ins to assess control progress

Important: Rodent control effectiveness depends significantly on exclusion work. We provide detailed recommendations for sealing entry points, which property owners can address themselves or hire a contractor to complete.

Typical Program: Initial treatment with traps/bait stations, followed by 2-3 monitoring visits over 4-6 weeks. Seasonal reactivation recommended before fall.

Cockroach Treatment

Cockroaches can be challenging to eliminate without professional help. They reproduce quickly, hide in hard-to-reach areas, and can spread bacteria and allergens. Our cockroach treatment program uses a combination of methods for effective control.

Common Species:

German Cockroaches: Small, fast-reproducing; common in kitchens and bathrooms
American Cockroaches: Large; often found in basements, crawl spaces, and sewers
Oriental Cockroaches: Dark colored; prefer damp areas like basements

Treatment Methods:

  • Gel bait applications in harborage areas
  • Crack and crevice treatments with residual insecticides
  • Growth regulators to disrupt reproduction cycles
  • Sanitation and prevention recommendations
  • Follow-up visits to ensure control and reapply as needed

Customer Cooperation Required: Effective cockroach control requires maintaining cleanliness, removing clutter, and addressing moisture issues. We provide detailed instructions for preparation and ongoing prevention.

Wasp & Stinging Insect Removal

Wasps, hornets, and bees can pose serious risks, especially to individuals with allergies. Our stinging insect removal services address nests in various locations around your property, from eaves and attics to ground nests and tree branches.

Types of Stinging Insects:

  • Yellow Jackets: Aggressive defenders; build paper nests in walls, eaves, and ground
  • Paper Wasps: Less aggressive but will defend nests; umbrella-shaped nests under eaves
  • Bald-Faced Hornets: Large nests in trees and shrubs; very protective of nest area
  • Carpenter Bees: Bore holes in wood; males are territorial but cannot sting

Our Service Includes:

  • Nest location and species identification
  • Direct treatment of active nests
  • Nest removal when safely accessible
  • Preventative treatments to deter re-nesting

Safety Note: Stinging insect removal can be dangerous. Treatment is typically performed in early morning or evening when insects are less active. Customers should remain indoors during service.

Spider Control & Web Removal

While most spiders in the Hudson Falls area are harmless, they can be a nuisance and some people have significant concerns about spider presence in their homes. Our spider control service reduces spider populations and removes webs from both interior and exterior areas.

Treatment Approach:

  • De-webbing of eaves, overhangs, and entry areas
  • Perimeter treatment to create a barrier
  • Interior treatment in basements and crawl spaces
  • Reduction of prey insects that attract spiders

Spider control works best as part of a comprehensive pest management program, as reducing other insects that spiders feed on helps create lasting results.

Good to Know: Most spiders in our area are beneficial predators that eat other insects. We focus on reducing populations in living areas while preserving beneficial spider activity in appropriate outdoor locations.

Termite Inspection Services

Termite damage can be costly and extensive if left undetected. Our termite inspection service provides a comprehensive evaluation of your property to identify signs of termite activity, conducive conditions, and existing damage.

Inspection Includes:

  • Visual inspection of accessible areas (foundation, basement, crawl space, attic)
  • Identification of termite mud tubes and damage
  • Assessment of moisture and wood-to-soil contact issues
  • Written inspection report with photos
  • Treatment recommendations if activity is found

Real Estate Transactions: We provide termite inspection reports suitable for real estate closings. Contact us for specific documentation requirements.

Note: Termite treatment (if needed) is quoted separately after inspection. Treatment methods may include liquid barrier treatments, bait systems, or localized wood treatments depending on findings.
